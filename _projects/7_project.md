Project page: [HAID](https://fitushar.github.io/HAID.github.io/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/fitushar/HAID)

HAID is an open-access repository providing 13+ standardized medical imaging datasets covering lung cancer, COVID-19, and universal lesions with expert-verified annotations, preprocessed files, and train-test splits. It democratizes access to high-quality, standardized clinical data — accelerating reproducible AI research across multiple disease domains.
